Frequently asked questions

  • What materials are the jewellery made of?

    Our jewellery is made of solid gold, silver or leather. Also, some jewellery contains precious and semiprecious stones and crystals.

  • Which countries do you deliver to?

    We deliver to a wide range of countries worldwide. To confirm whether we deliver to your specific location, please proceed to checkout where you can enter your address details for verification.

  • What are the payment methods?

    We offer several convenient payment methods to ensure a seamless shopping experience. These include credit/debit card payments, PayPal and bank transfers.

  • Can I return the jewellery if it didn’t fit me?

    Absolutely! We understand the importance of finding the perfect fit. If the jewellery you ordered doesn't fit as expected, you can return it within our specified return period. Please review our return policy for details on eligibility and procedures.
